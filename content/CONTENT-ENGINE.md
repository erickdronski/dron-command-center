---

## Voice & Tone

- **Direct, no fluff** — Get to the point in sentence one
- **Data-backed when possible** — Share real numbers
- **"Here's what I tried"** not "AI will change everything"
- **Occasional humor/dark takes** — You're human, not a corporate account
- **Confident but not arrogant** — "This worked for me" not "This is the only way"

## Signal vs Noise Guidelines

### Post-Worthy (Do Post)
- Real results with numbers
- Counter-intuitive findings
- Something you tried that failed
- Clear before/after
- Specific tactics with context
- Honest limitations

### Skip (Don't Post)
- Generic AI hype without specifics
- "AI is changing everything" takes without evidence
- Reposting news without your angle
- Motivational fluff
- Predictions without skin in the game

## Engagement Strategy

### Posting Best Practices
- **Best times:** Tuesday-Thursday, 8-10 AM EST
- **Length:** 150-300 words (LinkedIn sweet spot)
- **Formatting:** Use line breaks, bullets, arrows
- **Hashtags:** 3-5 max, mix broad (#AI) and specific (#OpenClaw)
- **CTA:** Optional, but "What's your experience?" drives comments

### Engagement Tactics
- Reply to every comment in first hour
- Ask follow-up questions to drive conversation
- Tag relevant people when appropriate
- Cross-post to X with slight modifications
