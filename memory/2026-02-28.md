### Contour Rings — The Fight
**Problem:** Fixed `<div>` overlay (z-index 9990, `mix-blend-mode: multiply`) blocked by solid section backgrounds even at high z-index.

**Solution:** Removed the div; switched to `background-attachment: fixed` + `background-image: url('/bg-contour.svg')` on `section` and `main > header` in `globals.css`. Used `!important` to override inline background shorthand.

**Status at session end:** Deployed (commit `bc7b93c`), user had not yet confirmed rings visible site-wide.

**If rings still broken:** Try `mix-blend-mode: darken` or `overlay`, or embed SVG directly into each cream section.
